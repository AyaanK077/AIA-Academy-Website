Moving away for college was a big adjustment. Here are three things that helped me
stay connected to my deen:

1. **Find the MSA early.** The Muslim Students' Association became my second family.
2. **Anchor your day around salah.** Blocking prayer times in my calendar kept everything else in balance.
3. **Stay in touch with home.** Calling family and old ISNS friends reminded me who I am.

To the graduating class: you've got this, and the whole ISNS family is rooting for you.
