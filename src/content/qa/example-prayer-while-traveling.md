A traveler (musafir) may shorten the four-rak'ah prayers to two and may combine
Dhuhr with Asr, and Maghrib with Isha, when traveling the distance that qualifies
as a journey. For your specific situation, please consult your local scholar, as
rulings can depend on the details of distance, duration, and intention.
